Rendered entirely with my own renderer, Takua Render. More information is available on my project pages and blog.

Born in Hannover, Germany and raised in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Yining Karl Li is a computer graphics software engineer at Walt Disney Animation Studios working on Disney's Hyperion Renderer.

Karl is a Master of Science in Computer Graphics candidate at Cornell University's Program of Computer Graphics and did his undergraduate studies at the University of Pennsylvania. Karl has also worked at Pixar Animation Studios and Dreamworks Animation.
